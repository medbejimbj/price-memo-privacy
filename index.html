Privacy Policy

Price Memo - Scan & Compare Store Prices

Last updated: May 22, 2026

Your personal data (lists, prices, products) stays on your device. Anonymous usage analytics are collected to improve the app (no personal info, can be disabled in app settings — coming in V2.7).

Data Storage

All data you enter in Price Memo (products, prices, stores) is stored locally on your device using an embedded database. No data is sent to any external server.

No Account Required

Price Memo does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to use the app.

Network Usage

Price Memo connects to the internet only for the following purposes:

  • Product identification: When you scan a barcode, the app queries OpenFoodFacts (an open-source database) to retrieve product name, brand, and image.
  • Store names: When you select a country, the app downloads store locations from OpenStreetMap to help you quickly enter store names.
  • Community price suggestions: When viewing or adding a price for a product, the app may query Open Prices (a public community database run by the OpenFoodFacts foundation) to suggest prices reported by other users. Queries are filtered by country and approximate location only.

These requests are anonymous and do not include any personal information.

Permissions

  • Camera: Used solely for barcode scanning. No photos are stored or transmitted.
  • Location: Used optionally to calculate distances to stores near you. Your location is never recorded or shared.

Analytics & Usage Data (Firebase Analytics) — V2.6.3+

To improve the app and optimize our advertising campaigns (Google Ads), we collect anonymous usage data via Google Firebase Analytics:

  • Usage events: product scans, list creations, optimizer use, price additions (no personal content)
  • Technical identifiers: Android Advertising ID (AAID), device model, OS version, country, app version
  • Sessions: first open, session duration, crashes

Purpose: improve the app and optimize Google Ads campaigns.
Legal basis: legitimate interest / consent (depending on your country).
Recipient: Google Ireland Ltd + transfer to USA (Standard Contractual Clauses).
Retention: 14 months.
Your rights: access, rectification, deletion. Complaint to your data protection authority (e.g. CNIL for France: cnil.fr).

No personally identifiable information (name, email, photos, lists) is sent. A consent toggle will be available in app Settings > Privacy in V2.7.

No Advertising in the App

Price Memo contains no in-app advertisements and no third-party SDKs other than Firebase Analytics (above).

Children's Privacy

Price Memo does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy is updated, changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.
